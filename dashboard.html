🔒 Federal Fraud Complaint Guidelines

CONFIDENTIALITY

All information shared during the complaint process is strictly confidential and protected under federal privacy laws. Your personal and financial information will only be shared with authorized investigative agencies.

CUSTOMER DECLARATION

By filing a fraud complaint, you declare under penalty of perjury that all information provided is true and accurate to the best of your knowledge. False statements may result in legal consequences.

⚠️ DEVICE SECURITY WARNING

DOUBLE CHECK YOUR DEVICES - If your device is getting heated frequently with or without charging, it may be infected with viruses or malware through compromised WiFi networks. Launch complaints immediately for:

  • Unusual device behavior or overheating
  • Suspicious network activity
  • Unauthorized access to accounts
  • Malware or virus infections

🛡️ SECURITY & SAFETY NOTICE

IF YOU HAVE BEEN HACKED or your information has been compromised, ensure all shared information remains confidential between you and investigative officers for your safety, security, and reimbursement of loss.

ASSET VERIFICATION & INSURANCE

Launch your complaint by filling the Asset Verification Form for any loss or reimbursement processing. This is required for security purposes and insurance claims processing.

Reimbursement Processing: Once your asset verification is submitted, our team will review your claim and process reimbursement according to insurance policies and federal guidelines. Processing typically takes 5-7 business days for approved claims.

REFERENCE SECRET NUMBER

Keep your reference secret number secure and confidential. This number is used to track your complaint and verify your identity during follow-up communications.
